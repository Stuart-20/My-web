Ministry home

Uganda is a home to more then 50 ethnic groups, each with its ownn language, dress, customs, and cultural practices. Some of the largest groups include Buganda, Banyankole, Basoga, Bakiga, Iteso, Acholi. and Banyarwanda. This cultural diversity makes Uganda one of the most culturally rich in Africa. Tourists have the chance to witness different ceremonies, dances, as well as lifestyles when they visit various regions of the country
